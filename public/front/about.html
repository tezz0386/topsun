OUR STORY

It stands on its own name 'The Sun on the Top' and it is always shining above the clouds which is the only source of the world and never destroys or disappears until the dooms day of the universe.

Topsun Energy Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company and is established to become one of the leading companies working in the field of Renewal Energy Sector. It is registered in the year 2010 A.D. (2067 B.S.). Since from the inception, this company believes the Electricity as a strong means of development, and is contributing its effort by providing alternative products for the generation of electricity. Among them, it is promoting solar energy sector at its best.

Topsun Energy Private Limited presents the market with reliable, cheap and convenient solar solutions for the people suffering from load shedding problems and not access to electricity. Our company deals with all parts of solar components and we basically serve solar facilities from survey to installation. It is a Pre Qualified company from AEPC for ISPS, PVPS, SHS and SSHS and has installed numbers of ISPS systems as well as KW projects inside and outside the valley.

It has the vast experience in installation of ISPS, URBAN ISPS, STREET LIGHT and PV PUMPING SYSTEM. A part from this it has already experienced successful installation of Photo Voltaic System in different sectors like Hospitals, Schools, Co-operatives and FM stations and Government Offices at different locations of the Country. Our Solar Photovoltaic systems are specially designed for your use. Our systems directly connect to your existing wiring and power up all your necessary appliances.

Because unlike other companies, TOPSUN ENERGY PVT. LTD. is able to get most of its supplies directly from the manufacturer at a wholesale price, so we believe our products and services are much lower than our competitors. Furthermore, we always guarantee the high quality of our product and are proud to give such guarantee because we believe on our product and experiences the performance of the product since from the inception of the company.
